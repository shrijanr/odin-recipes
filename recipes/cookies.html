Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Indulge your sweet tooth with these irresistible homemade chocolate chip cookies. This classic recipe combines the perfect balance of buttery goodness, rich dark chocolate chips, and a hint of vanilla for a chewy and decadent treat that will leave you craving more. With a quick and easy preparation process, you'll have warm, freshly baked cookies straight from the oven in no time, filling your kitchen with that delightful aroma. Whether you're baking these for a special occasion or just to satisfy your sweet cravings, these chocolate chip cookies are sure to be a hit with friends and family alike. Enjoy them with a glass of cold milk for the ultimate comfort food experience.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Nuts

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown
