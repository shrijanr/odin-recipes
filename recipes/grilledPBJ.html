Grilled PBJ

Description

Elevate your classic peanut butter and jelly sandwich by giving it a delightful twist with a grilled peanut butter and jelly sandwich. Spread a generous layer of creamy peanut butter and your favorite fruit jelly between two slices of soft bread, then butter the outsides of the sandwich for that perfect golden brown crust. Heat up a skillet or a pan over medium heat, and place the sandwich in the pan. Grill it until it's crispy and golden on both sides, and the peanut butter and jelly inside are warm and gooey. The heat transforms the traditional flavors into a melty, savory-sweet delight that's perfect for a quick and satisfying meal or snack. Cut it diagonally for that nostalgic childhood feel, and enjoy the irresistible combination of warm, toasty bread and the comforting flavors of peanut butter and jelly.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp butter
  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 tsp any fruit jelly
  • 1 tsp peanut butter

Steps

  1. Heat a griddle or skillet to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread. Spread jelly on the unbuttered side of one slice of bread; spread peanut butter on the other unbuttered side
  3. Place bread slices together, so peanut butter and jelly are in the middle.
  4. Cook on the preheated griddle until golden brown and heated through, about 4 minutes per side.
  5. Enjoy
